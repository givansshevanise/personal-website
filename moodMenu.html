The Problem

People struggle to decide what to cook‚Äînot because they lack recipes, but because existing recipe apps don't account for their current emotional state or energy level.

The Challenge

Someone exhausted after work doesn't want to scroll through 30-minute recipes. Someone seeking therapeutic cooking doesn't want microwave meals.

Decision fatigue around meals leads to unhealthy eating habits, food waste, and increased stress. 73% of people feel overwhelmed by meal decisions weekly.
